Diamond Jubilee Hall Renovation

Restoring an iconic landmark that has witnessed generations of memories, celebrations, and achievements.

Project Overview

The Diamond Jubilee Hall has been the venue for countless cultural events, graduation ceremonies, technical festivals, alumni gatherings, and student activities. Through this renovation initiative, CETAA aims to modernize the hall while preserving its rich heritage.

Modern Interiors

Renovating seating arrangements, stage, lighting, and interior finishes for a world-class experience.

Advanced Facilities

Installation of modern sound systems, LED lighting, air-conditioning, and digital presentation facilities.

Preserving Heritage

Maintaining the historic identity of the hall while incorporating modern infrastructure.
